## 🆘 Common First-Day Questions

### "What if I don't know how to do an exercise?"
- Watch a quick YouTube video before the gym
- Ask gym staff for help (they're there to help!)
- Start light, focus on form over weight
- Better to go light with good form than heavy with bad form

### "What if the gym is crowded?"
- Have backup exercises ready (e.g., dumbbell squats instead of leg press)
- Wait politely for equipment
- Consider going slightly earlier or later if possible
- Remember: everyone started somewhere, no one is judging

### "What if I'm too tired after work?"
- This is normal! Energy comes AFTER you start
- Just commit to 10 minutes - you'll usually continue
- Remember your "why" (better job + better body + more energy for family)
- Change immediately when you get home (don't sit on couch first)

### "What if I can't hit 140g protein?"
- Don't stress on Day 1
- Add an extra whey scoop
- Eat Greek yogurt before bed
- Aim for progress, not perfection

### "What if I forget to track something?"
- Log it as soon as you remember
- Missing one data point ≠ failure
- Consistency over time matters more than perfect tracking
