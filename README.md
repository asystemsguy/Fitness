## Primary Goal

**Lose weight and reduce body fat (especially stomach area) by building muscle**

- Strength training boosts metabolism for hours after workout
- Building muscle while losing fat = better body composition
- Target: Lose 0.5-1 kg per week (sustainable, maintains muscle)

### Target

## Workout Templates

### Option A: Home Bodyweight (20-30 min)
```
Warmup (5 min):
- Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists
- 20 jumping jacks

Circuit (repeat 3x):
- Push-ups: 10-15 reps
- Squats: 15-20 reps
- Lunges: 10 each leg
- Plank: 30-45 seconds
- Burpees: 5-10 reps

Cooldown (5 min):
- Stretch major muscle groups
```

### Option B: Gym Session (45-60 min)
```
Warmup (5-10 min):
- Light cardio + dynamic stretching

Strength:
- Compound lifts (squats, deadlifts, bench, rows)
- 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

Cardio (optional):
- 15-20 min moderate intensity

Cooldown:
- Stretching
```

### Option C: Quick Session (10-15 min)
```
For busy days - something is better than nothing!

- 5 min warmup
- 3 rounds: 20 squats, 10 push-ups, 30s plank
- 2 min stretch
```

---

## Principles

1. **Consistency > Intensity** - Show up, even if it's a short workout
2. **Progressive Overload** - Gradually increase difficulty
3. **Rest & Recovery** - Muscles grow during rest, not workouts
4. **Listen to Your Body** - Adjust based on energy levels
5. **No Guilt** - Missed a day? Just continue tomorrow
