# Weekly Fitness Planner

## How to Use
- Plan your workouts at the START of each week (Sunday evening)
- Adjust as needed during the week (life happens!)
- Review at week's end - what worked, what didn't

## Quick Workouts for Busy Weeks

When life gets crazy, use these:

### 10-Minute Emergency Workout
```
- 2 min warmup (jumping jacks, arm circles)
- 3 rounds: 10 push-ups, 15 squats, 20s plank
- 2 min stretch
```

### 5-Minute Mobility
```
- Cat-cow stretches: 1 min
- Hip circles: 1 min
- Shoulder rolls: 1 min
- Torso twists: 1 min
- Deep breathing: 1 min
```

### Walk It Out
```
- 15-20 min brisk walk
- Better than nothing!
```
