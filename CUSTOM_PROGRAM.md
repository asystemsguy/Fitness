**Goal:** Build muscle + lose fat (especially stomach)  
**Schedule:** Evenings after 6 PM  
**Equipment:** Home (20lb dumbbells) + Apartment Gym

---

## Why We're Adjusting Your Current Routine

**What You Were Doing Well:**
✅ Full-body compound movements (great for metabolism)  
✅ Interval structure (40s work / 20s rest = keeps heart rate up)  
✅ 30-minute duration (sustainable, fits schedule)  
✅ Mix of strength + core work  

**What We're Adding for Better Results:**
🎯 **Progressive overload tracking** - You need to track reps/weights to ensure you're getting stronger  
🎯 **Heavier lifting at gym** - 20lb dumbbells are maintenance; gym equipment lets you build more muscle  
🎯 **Dedicated cardio sessions** - Separate cardio days maximize fat loss without interfering with muscle building  
🎯 **Rest days** - Muscles grow during recovery, not workouts

## Gym Workouts (Monday & Thursday)

### Strength A (Monday) - Lower Body Focus

**Warmup (5 min):**
- Treadmill walk: 3 min
- Dynamic stretches: leg swings, hip circles, bodyweight squats

**Main Lifts (track your weights!):**

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| Goblet Squats (dumbbell or machine) | 4 | 10-12 | 90s | Go heavy, last 2 reps challenging |
| Romanian Deadlifts (barbell or dumbbells) | 4 | 10-12 | 90s | Feel stretch in hamstrings |
| Leg Press | 3 | 12-15 | 60s | Control the descent |
| Walking Lunges (with dumbbells) | 3 | 12 each leg | 60s | Keep torso upright |
| Calf Raises | 3 | 15-20 | 45s | Full range of motion |

**Cardio Finisher:**
- Treadmill: 15 min incline walk (speed 5-6 km/h, incline 8-12%)
- OR Cycling: 15 min moderate resistance

**Cooldown (5 min):**
- Quad stretch, hamstring stretch, hip flexor stretch

### Strength B (Thursday) - Upper Body + Core Focus

**Warmup (5 min):**
- Arm circles, shoulder rolls, cat-cow

**Main Lifts (track your weights!):**

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |

**Cardio Finisher:**

**Cooldown (5 min):**
- Chest stretch, lat stretch, tricep stretch

## Home Workouts (Tuesday & Friday)

### Dumbbell Circuit (Your Oliver Sjostrom Routine)

**Structure:** 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest

| Block | Exercises | Duration |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Warmup | Arm circles, side bends, trunk twists, runner's stretch | 3 min |
| Block 1 | Sumo squat + hammer curl + back rows | 4 min |
| Block 2 | Deadlifts + squats + overhead tricep extensions | 4 min |
| Block 3 | Side lunges + lateral raises + bicep curl-to-press | 4 min |
| Block 4 | Leg raises + glute bridges + chest presses | 4 min |
| Block 5 | Side planks + push-ups + mountain climbers | 4 min |
| Block 6 | Renegade rows + reverse lunges with curls | 4 min |
| Cooldown | Knee hugs, hamstring stretches, butterfly stretch | 3 min |

**Total:** 30 minutes

**Progression:** 
- Week 1-2: Complete all rounds with good form
- Week 3-4: Increase reps in each 40s window
- Week 5-6: Reduce rest to 15s OR add a 4th round
- Week 7-8: Use heavier dumbbells at gym before home sessions

**How to Progress:**
- If you hit the top of the rep range (e.g., 12 reps) with good form → increase weight next session
- If you can't hit the bottom of the rep range (e.g., only 8 reps) → decrease weight or stay same
- Aim to add 2.5-5 lbs every 1-2 weeks on major lifts

### Home Days (Dumbbells)
| Week | Goal |
|------|------|
| 1-2 | Complete all blocks with good form |
| 3-4 | Increase reps in each 40s window |
| 5-6 | Reduce rest to 15s between blocks |
| 7-8 | Add 4th round to blocks 1-3 |

## Nutrition for Fat Loss + Muscle Gain

**Calorie Target:** ~2000-2200 kcal/day (moderate deficit)

**Macronutrient Split:**
- **Protein:** 140-160g/day (preserves muscle during fat loss)
- **Carbs:** 200-250g/day (fuels workouts)
- **Fats:** 60-80g/day (hormone health, satiety)

**Simple Meal Framework:**

| Meal | Protein | Carbs | Vegetables |
|------|---------|-------|------------|
| Breakfast | 2-3 eggs / Greek yogurt | 1 slice bread / oats | Handful of spinach |
| Lunch | Chicken / fish / paneer (150g) | Rice / roti (1 cup) | Large portion |
| Snack | Protein shake / nuts | Fruit | - |
| Dinner | Lean protein (150g) | Small portion carbs | Large portion |

**Pre-Workout (30-60 min before evening workout):**
- Light carb + protein snack
- Examples: Banana + handful of almonds, or Greek yogurt + berries

**Post-Workout (within 2 hours after):**
- Protein + carbs for recovery
- Examples: Chicken + rice, or protein shake + fruit

**Hydration:**
- 3-4 liters water/day
- More on workout days

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Only doing the home dumbbell workouts** - You need heavier weights at the gym for optimal muscle building  
❌ **Skipping cardio** - Cardio accelerates fat loss and improves heart health  
❌ **Not tracking weights/reps** - How will you know you're progressing?  
❌ **Expecting stomach fat to disappear first** - Fat loss is overall, not spot-specific  
❌ **Working out every day** - Muscles need rest to grow  
❌ **Eating too little** - You need fuel for workouts and muscle recovery  

✅ **Do:** Gym 2x + Home 2x + Cardio 2x + Rest 2x + Track everything + Eat enough protein

## Quick Reference: Your 20lb Dumbbells

**20lb = ~9 kg** (each dumbbell)

**What You Can Do at Home:**
- Maintenance workouts when you can't make it to the gym
- Metabolic conditioning (your Oliver Sjostrom routine)
- Core work + light upper body
- Travel workouts

**Limitations:**
- Too light for progressive overload on major lifts (squats, deadlifts, rows)
- You'll plateau quickly if you ONLY use these

**Strategy:**
- Use gym for heavy compound lifts (progressive overload)
- Use home dumbbells for conditioning and maintenance
- Goal: Eventually buy adjustable dumbbells for home if gym becomes inconvenient
