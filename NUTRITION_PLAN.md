## Weekly Meal Prep Guide

### Sunday Prep (30-45 minutes)

**1. Protein Prep:**
- Boil 6-8 eggs (store in fridge, peel as needed)
- Marinate chicken/fish for Mon-Wed dinners
- Cut paneer into cubes for grilling

**2. Carb Prep:**
- Cook 2-3 cups rice (portion and refrigerate)
- Make roti dough (store in fridge, roll fresh daily)

**3. Snack Prep:**
- Portion nuts into small bags (10-12 almonds each)
- Wash and cut fruits
- Make Greek yogurt portions

**4. Vegetable Prep:**
- Wash and chop vegetables for 2-3 days
- Store in airtight containers

---

## Cooking Tips for Fat Loss

### Do's ✅
- Use minimal oil (1-2 tsp per dish)
- Choose tomato-onion gravies over cream-based
- Grill, bake, or air-fry instead of deep-frying
- Use non-stick pans to reduce oil
- Load up on vegetables (fill half your plate)
- Choose whole wheat roti over maida (refined flour)
- Use low-fat dairy (Greek yogurt, low-fat paneer)

### Don'ts ❌
- Avoid or minimize: samosa, pakora, puri, paratha (excess oil)
- Limit: sweets, mithai, sugary drinks
- Reduce: white rice portions (mix with brown or cauliflower rice)
- Avoid: late-night heavy meals (after 9 PM)
- Minimize: restaurant food (hidden calories, excess oil)

---

## Eating Out / Social Events

### At Restaurants:
- Choose: Grilled/tandoori items (chicken tikka, fish tikka)
- Choose: Dal, sabzi, salad
- Limit: Butter chicken, korma, biryani (high calorie)
- Skip: Naan (choose roti instead)
- Skip: Desserts (or share one)
- Drink: Water, avoid sugary drinks and excess alcohol

### At Parties/Events:
- Eat a light protein snack before going (prevents overeating)
- Fill plate with: Protein (chicken/paneer) + vegetables
- Limit: 1 small portion of carbs (rice/roti)
- Avoid standing near the food table
- Sip water throughout

## Hydration

**Daily Target:** 3-4 liters water

**Timing:**
- 1 glass upon waking (kickstarts metabolism)
- 1 glass before each meal (helps with portion control)
- 1 glass during workout
- 1 glass post-workout
- Sip throughout the day

**Signs You're Not Drinking Enough:**
- Dark yellow urine
- Headaches
- Fatigue
- Increased hunger (thirst masks as hunger)

**Tips:**
- Keep water bottle at desk
- Set phone reminders
- Drink 1 glass before coffee/tea
- Add lemon/cucumber for flavor (no calories)

## Supplement Guide

### You Have: Whey Protein ✓

**When to Take:**
- Post-workout (best time): 1 scoop in water
- Breakfast: Mix into oats or smoothie
- Anytime you're short on protein for the day

**How Much:**
- 1-2 scoops per day (depending on food intake)
- Don't exceed 2 scoops (food first, supplements second)

### Optional Supplements (Not Required)

**Creatine Monohydrate (3-5g/day):**
- Helps with strength and muscle building
- Safe, well-researched
- Mix with water or post-workout shake

**Multivitamin:**
- Insurance policy for micronutrients
- Take with breakfast

**Vitamin D3:**
- Many Indians are deficient
- Especially if you don't get much sun
- 1000-2000 IU/day

**Omega-3 (Fish Oil):**
- Good for heart health, inflammation
- 1-2g/day
- Especially if you don't eat fish regularly

**NOT Needed (Save Your Money):**
- Fat burners (scam)
- BCAAs (whey already has them)
- Pre-workout (caffeine from coffee works)
- Testosterone boosters (scam)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Eating too little** - You need fuel for workouts and muscle. Aim for 2000+ calories.

❌ **Not enough protein** - Muscle won't build/repair. Hit 140g+ daily.

❌ **All-or-nothing thinking** - One "bad" meal doesn't ruin progress. Get back on track next meal.

❌ **Drinking calories** - Avoid sugary drinks, excess juice, alcohol (empty calories).

❌ **Late-night eating** - Finish dinner by 8-8:30 PM when possible.

❌ **Skipping pre-workout nutrition** - You'll feel weak during workouts.

❌ **Not tracking** - Use an app (MyFitnessPal, HealthifyMe) for first 2 weeks to learn portions.

❌ **Comparing to others** - Your friend's diet might not work for you. Stick to your plan.

### Weekly Check-in (Every Sunday)

| Metric | Target | This Week |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Weight | Lose 0.5-1 kg/week | ___ kg |
| Workouts | 4-5/week | ___/5 |

| Water | 3L+/day | ___L |
| Sleep | 7-8 hours | ___ hrs |

### Adjustments Based on Progress

**If losing >1.5 kg/week:**
- Increase calories by 200-300 (add carbs/fats)
- You're losing too fast (might lose muscle)

**If losing <0.5 kg/week (after 2 weeks):**
- Reduce calories by 200 (cut carbs slightly)
- OR increase cardio by 10 min/session

**If strength is decreasing:**
- Increase protein by 20g/day
- Ensure you're eating enough carbs for workouts
- Check sleep quality

**If constantly hungry:**
- Increase vegetables (fiber, volume)
- Increase protein slightly
- Drink more water

## Mindset & Sustainability

**Remember:**
- This is a **lifestyle**, not a 30-day challenge
- **80/20 rule:** Eat well 80% of time, 20% flexibility
- **Progress > Perfection:** One bad meal doesn't matter
- **Consistency > Intensity:** Small daily habits beat occasional extremes

**When You Slip Up:**
1. Don't guilt-trip yourself (counterproductive)
2. Ask: "What triggered this?" (stress? hunger? social?)
3. Plan how to handle it next time
4. Get back on track at the NEXT meal (not tomorrow)

**Celebrating Wins:**
- Non-scale victories matter (energy, strength, clothes fitting better)
- Don't use food as reward (use new workout gear, massage, etc.)
